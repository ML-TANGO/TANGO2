With a head-on target detected on radar in dawn fog with restricted visibility, immediately reduce to safe speed (Rule 6) and keep engines ready for immediate maneuvering in accordance with COLREG Rule 19. Sound fog signals of one prolonged blast at intervals of not more than two minutes as required by Rule 35, and continuously monitor the target through radar plotting. If risk of collision is confirmed, alter course to starboard by a substantial margin in ample time. A port turn is strictly prohibited in a head-on situation.
