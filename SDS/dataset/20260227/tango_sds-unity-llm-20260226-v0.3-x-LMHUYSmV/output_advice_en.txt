With a high-speed crossing target detected on radar on the port bow in dawn fog, immediately reduce to safe speed (Rule 6) and sound fog signals per Rule 35 in accordance with Rule 19. As required by Rule 19(d)(i), avoid altering course to port for a vessel forward of the beam, and continuously calculate CPA through radar plotting. If risk of collision exists, secure a safe passing distance by altering course to starboard or substantially reducing speed.
