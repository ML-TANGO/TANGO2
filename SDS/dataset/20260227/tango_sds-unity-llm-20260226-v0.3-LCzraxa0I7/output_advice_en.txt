With an overtaking situation detected on radar in dawn fog with restricted visibility, immediately reduce to safe speed (Rule 6) and sound fog signals of one prolonged blast at two-minute intervals per Rule 35 in accordance with Rule 19. Continuously monitor the target's CPA through radar plotting, and ensure a CPA of at least 1 nautical mile before passing. Alter course to pass at a safe distance, and be prepared to reduce speed further if the situation develops unfavorably.
