This is a Crossing Situation with the target vessel on the starboard bow. In accordance with COLREG Rule 15, own vessel is the give-way vessel and must avoid crossing ahead of the target by making a substantial alteration of course to starboard or reducing speed to pass safely astern. Action must be taken early and positively, and the target's bearing change must be closely monitored until a minimum CPA of 1 nautical mile is secured.
