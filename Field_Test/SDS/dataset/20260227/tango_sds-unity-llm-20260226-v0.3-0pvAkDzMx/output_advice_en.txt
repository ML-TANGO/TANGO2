This is an Overtaking Situation where own vessel is overtaking the target. In accordance with COLREG Rule 13, own vessel is the give-way vessel and must maintain sufficient safe passing distance (CPA of at least 1 nautical mile) without impeding the target's course. Take particular care not to cross ahead or approach too closely to avoid ship-to-ship interaction effects, and maintain vigilance until the overtaking maneuver is fully completed.
