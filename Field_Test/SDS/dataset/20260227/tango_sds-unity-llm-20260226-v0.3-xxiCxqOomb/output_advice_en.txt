With a head-on target detected on radar in severely restricted dawn fog visibility, immediately reduce to safe speed (Rule 6) and keep engines ready in accordance with Rule 19. Sound fog signals of one prolonged blast at two-minute intervals per Rule 35, and continuously calculate the target's CPA and TCPA through radar plotting. If risk of collision is determined, alter course to starboard by a substantial angle early. Altering course to port must be avoided.
