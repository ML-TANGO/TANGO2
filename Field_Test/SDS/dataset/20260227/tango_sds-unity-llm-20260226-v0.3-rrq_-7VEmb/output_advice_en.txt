With a high-speed head-on target detected on radar in extremely restricted dawn fog visibility, immediately reduce speed substantially to safe speed (Rule 6) and keep engines ready in accordance with Rule 19. Sound fog signals per Rule 35, and urgently calculate CPA and TCPA through radar plotting. With a combined closing speed exceeding 44 knots, immediately execute a substantial starboard course alteration to ensure sufficient safe distance. Altering course to port is strictly prohibited.
